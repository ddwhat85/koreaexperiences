Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Korea experiences, operated by OOMAILAB, handles information when you use www.koreaexperiences.com.

1. Information we collect

We aim to collect as little personal data as possible. When you make a purchase, payment and billing information (such as your email address and payment details) is collected and processed by our payment provider, Paddle.com, not stored on our servers. We store a small record of your purchase credits locally in your browser (via localStorage) so the Service knows how many AI trip generations remain.

2. Third-party services

The Service uses third-party providers to function, including: Paddle (payment processing), Google Gemini (AI itinerary generation), and the Korea Tourism Organization TourAPI and related public data sources (travel information). Your use of the Service may involve data being processed by these providers under their own privacy policies.

3. Cookies and local storage

We use browser local storage to remember your purchase credits and basic preferences. We do not use advertising trackers to build profiles of you.

4. How we use information

We use information solely to operate and improve the Service, process payments through Paddle, and respond to support requests.

5. Data retention

Purchase credit data is stored in your own browser and can be cleared by you at any time through your browser settings. Payment records are retained by Paddle as required for their services and legal obligations.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data held about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at ddwhat85@gmail.com.

7. Children

The Service is not intended for children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at ddwhat85@gmail.com.
